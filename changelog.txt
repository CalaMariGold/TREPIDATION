§lTREPIDATION v0.2.4§r
§l§nThe Speedrunning Update!§r§r

§eRead the detailed changelog by clicking on the version number on the main menu.§r

§a§lMods§r§r
- Added Corpse Complex
- Added Opticheck
- Added Shut Up Model Loader
- Removed Death Counter
- Removed Realistic Torches

§a§Major Fixes§r§r
- Blocks mined within the first 10 seconds of starting a world now drop! 

§a§lDeath Rework§r§r
- Armor and baubles are now kept on death.
- Torches are now kept on death.
- The Soul Compass is now automatically given to the player on death.
- The Soul Compass can no longer be crafted or found.
- Enabled the Soulbinding enchantment. Items with this enchantment are kept on death.
- Dying after the timer reaches zero will now turn the player into a ghost for all of eternity.

§a§lSpeedrunning§r§r
- When escaping a dimension for the first time, necessary information for speedrunners are now outputted to chat. This includes:
- - The time left as soon as you entered the dimension
- - The elapsed time of the run so far
- - The amount of Timer Bonuses you used in the run so far
- - The seed of the world

§a§lMisc§r§r
- Decreased volume of one of the surface world songs.
- Gave some items the appropriate tooltip border.
- Buglins should no longer spawn outside of the Surface world.
- Removed all redstone/redstone based items, hoes, and fishing rods from the game (sorry redstone users)
- Barriers no longer drop themselves when mined
- Updated mods
- Added Dart Monkey functionality
