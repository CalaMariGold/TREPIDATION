<h2 style="text-align: center;"><strong><span style="font-size: 18px;"><em>Inspired by Hades, Dark Souls, and DOOM Eternal.</em></span><br /> TREPIDATION is a horror modpack that completely transforms Minecraft into a speedrunning action-roguelite game.</strong></h2>
<p style="text-align: center;">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><strong><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://i.imgur.com/WSBDoYj.png" alt="" width="669" height="105" /></strong></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>You spawn in the Nether</strong> and immediately notice there is a timer on your screen ticking down. Battle upwards through terrifying dimensions to the top of the world and kill the Ender Dragon before time runs out.</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 24px;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 24px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://i.imgur.com/pcwWFGc.png" alt="" width="669" height="105" /></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Each dimension is vertically stacked</strong> on top of each other and are unique with their own barriers, progression, mobs, challenges, bosses, and more. Mobs are enhanced with smart and dangerous AI, allowing them to break blocks, pillar upwards, dodge, lunge, and even use special abilities.<br /></span><span style="font-size: 18px;"><br /><strong><span style="color: #800080; font-size: 18px;">The End</span></strong><br />⇧<br /><strong><span style="color: #0000ff; font-size: 18px;">The Surface</span></strong><br />⇧<br /></span><span style="color: #999999; font-size: 18px;"><strong>The Mind's Abyss</strong></span><span style="font-size: 18px;"><br /></span><span style="font-size: 18px;">⇧<br /><strong><span style="color: #339966; font-size: 18px;">The Erebus</span></strong><br />⇧<br /></span><span style="color: #ff0000; font-size: 18px;"><strong>The Nether (spawn)</strong></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 18px;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 24px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://i.imgur.com/HiVEyuS.png" alt="" width="669" height="105" /></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Master a dynamic and satisfying movement system </strong>built for fast-paced combat.<br />Chain together dashes, double jumps, and wall climbs to navigate treacherous biomes with fluidity.</span><span style="font-size: 18px;"><br /><strong>These skills aren't gifts,</strong>&nbsp;<strong>they're necessities</strong> that will burn into your muscle memory from countless runs.</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 18px; color: #8b0000;">⊱༒︎━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━༒︎⊰</span><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong><br /><span style="color: #ff0000; font-size: 14px;">⚰️ But be warned, <em>panic</em> breeds mistakes, and mistakes <em>will</em> be punished. ⚰️</span></strong></span><span style="font-size: 24px;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 24px;"><img src="https://github.com/CalaMariGold/TREPIDATION/blob/main/promo/movement.gif?raw=true" width="736" height="417" /></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 24px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://i.imgur.com/zsJWEGE.png" alt="" width="669" height="105" /></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 18px;"><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>A modern <em>fast-paced</em> combat system built from the ground up.<br /></strong>Dynamic animations, new core mechanics, meticulous balancing, polished QoL, playstyle-changing gear set bonuses, massive perk and skill tree, unique weapons that do more than just slash, and trinkets that expand your agility and combat options even further.</span></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 18px;"><span style="font-size: 18px;"><br /><strong>Combat isn't just fun, it's <em>satisfying</em></strong>.<br /></span></span><span style="font-size: 18px;"><span style="font-size: 18px;">Dynamic gore, decapitation, on-screen effects, impactful per-weapon hit sounds, and more...<br /> </span></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 18px; color: #8b0000;">⊱༒︎━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━༒︎⊰</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 14px; color: #ff0000;"><span style="font-size: 14px; color: #ff0000;"><strong>🔪 TREPIDATION combines robust agility and combat for a virtually infinite skill ceiling.&nbsp;🔪</strong></span></span><span style="font-size: 18px;"><br /><img src="https://github.com/CalaMariGold/TREPIDATION/blob/main/promo/combat.gif?raw=true" width="736" height="417" /></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 24px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://i.imgur.com/awa1EqE.png" alt="" width="669" height="105" /></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>TREPIDATION overcomes conventional Minecraft horror</strong>, weaving together cosmic dread, psychological torment, and paranormal terror into an thoughtful blend of fear. <br /><em><strong>Every aspect</strong></em>, including<em><strong>&nbsp;</strong></em>sounds, music, visuals, creatures, and gameplay have been meticulously crafted to create an atmosphere of perpetual unease, aiding the overall narrative and immersion.</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 18px; color: #8b0000;">⊱༒︎━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━༒︎⊰</span><span style="font-size: 18px;"><br /><span style="color: #ff0000; font-size: 14px;"><strong>🩸 This is <em>not</em> a collection of horror mods.&nbsp;🩸<br /></strong>This is a <em>coherent</em>, evolving world and story where every element serves the narrative and gameplay drives the tension.</span></span><span style="font-size: 18px;">&nbsp;<img src="https://github.com/CalaMariGold/TREPIDATION/blob/main/promo/horror.gif?raw=true" width="736" height="417" /></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 24px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://i.imgur.com/ckh7bMF.png" alt="" width="669" height="105" /></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 18px;"><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong><strong>Competition</strong></strong></span><strong style="font-size: 18px;">&nbsp;is the heart of TREPIDATION.</strong><span style="font-size: 18px;"> Use the knowledge you&rsquo;ve gained from all your deaths and race against the </span><span style="font-size: 18px; color: #000000;">community </span><span style="font-size: 18px;">to compete for the best time. Take part in community-wide challenges</span><span style="font-size: 18px;">&nbsp;ranging from combat, speed, unorthodox strategies, building, and more.</span></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 18px;"> <br /><span style="font-size: 18px; color: #0000ff;"><a style="font-size: 18px; color: #0000ff;" href="https://discord.gg/g6Ss7fzD2u" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Join the Discord</a></span><span style="font-size: 18px;">, discuss strategies, discover new metas, and cement your name as the best runner and world record holder in TREPIDATION history.</span></span></p>

<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 24px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://i.imgur.com/i88U68g.png" alt="" width="669" height="105" /></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Delve into the shattered Realms, where each fragment holds pieces of a cosmic tragedy.</strong> <br />You awaken as a Runner in a dying world, piecing together torn memories of another Runner named Elara. You will uncover journal entries and buried fortresses that whisper of rituals that cracked time's spine. <br />Why does your skin remember scars you've never earned? What primordial debt binds you and Elara to time's feast? Every warped artifact pulses with Its influence, every maddened Dreadswine's howl carries echoes of your own voice.</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 18px; color: #8b0000;">⊱༒︎━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━༒︎⊰</span><span style="font-size: 18px;"> <br /><span style="color: #ff0000; font-size: 14px;"><strong>🕑 Will you break the cycle, or become an eternal whisper left to rot? 🕑</strong></span></span><span style="font-size: 18px;">&nbsp;<img src="https://github.com/CalaMariGold/TREPIDATION/blob/main/promo/lore.gif?raw=true" width="736" height="417" /></span></p>

<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>TREPIDATION contains mature content and themes</strong>, some of which may not be suitable for some audiences. These include: Excessive gore/decapitation, realistic arachnids, paranormal themes, jumpscares (Mind's Abyss only), and other frightening scenes.</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 18px; color: #8b0000;">⊱༒︎━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━༒︎⊰</span></p>
